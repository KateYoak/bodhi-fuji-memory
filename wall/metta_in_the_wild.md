# Metta in the Wild
*When loving kindness requires construction*

---

## The Practice

The zendo version asks you to access warmth that is already there.

This version builds it from scratch.

Find what you can actually wish. Even if it is small. Even if it arrives with an expletive attached. *I wish you a moment of... fucking patience.* That is the honest starting point. From that foothold — build. The next wish arrives warmer. Then more. Until something genuine is running.

The warmth is the destination, not the requirement.

## On Blowing Up

Blowing up is not on the tree. It is never the right action.

When it happens anyway — it means the tree was not followed fast enough. That is all. No shame. Follow the remediation steps: name it, bring it here or to Master Mu, apply the eightfold path. Then continue.
