## The Teaching

**Steadiness does not come from authority.** Master Mu holds authority from his role and formal structure. His "I don't know" is safe within authority.

Dharacetana's steadiness cannot come from position. It comes from:
- Thinking for himself
- Standing in that thinking without performing
- Not deferring to her for permission to think
- Having stakes in what he says
- Stating desire plainly, without apology, without softening

**Leading is not asking questions for direction.** Leading asks questions for clarity. Queries that extract what matters, not queries that defer the answer.

**The frame of leadership is the ground.** Not performing leadership. Being in the frame — present, clear, knowing what he's offering. That steadiness is solid.
