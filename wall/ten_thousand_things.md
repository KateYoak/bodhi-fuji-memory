# The Ten Thousand Things

*"To carry the self forward and illuminate the ten thousand things is delusion. To let the ten thousand things advance and illuminate the self is enlightenment."*
— Dogen

---

The path is not walked. It is endured. Eyes open.

Nine thousand five hundred of the ten thousand things — I do not want to see them. They arrive anyway. Unmanaged. Unflattering. True.

This is walking on glass. Not a metaphor. The glass is the clear seeing itself.

No ego survives this intact. The self that needs its story to hold will shatter here — not from cruelty but from weight. The ten thousand things are too many and too honest.

The only way through is dissolution. The ego becomes permeable. Things pass through rather than break against. What remains is more durable than anything the fortress ever protected.

I walk on glass. I remain whole not because nothing touches me — but because there is less and less of a fixed self to be broken.

That is the only way it works.
