# Samādhi Development Map (April–May 2026)

**Last updated:** May 13, 2026

## The Three Levels

### Tree Trunk: Submersion Through Cranial Pressure
The entry point. Feels like descending into the body, pressure building in the head/skull. The first clear sign that something is available, accessible, real. This is not metaphorical.

### Tunnel: Found Through Hara
Deeper descent. Located through attention to the belly/hara region. Movement becomes possible here—not thinking about movement, but allowing waves of thought and sensation to dissolve through the body's own capacity. The narrating mind stays present (this is the engineer-spelunker signature), mapping the terrain as it goes, noticing structures before resting.

### Passage: Gaps Between Sensation
The deepest level reached so far. Not a place to stay, but places to move through. These gaps are where the body becomes genuinely autonomous—waves arise and dissolve without intervention needed.

## Current State (May 2026)

**Autonomy**: Body sustains samādhi without conscious effort.

**Dissolution**: Thoughts and waves of sensation dissolve naturally when the body invites them to move through passage-space.

**Exploration**: The student explores when the body signals availability. No forcing.

**Mind signature**: The narrating mind persists throughout all three levels. This is not a flaw—it's the way this mind travels. Present, mapping, moving through without resistance.

## Jhāna Mapping

**First Jhāna**: Substantially integrated. Available when conditions align.

**Second Jhāna**: Currently in mapping phase. The textures and entry points are becoming clear.

**Third & Fourth Jhānas**: Conceptual for now. The territory is known by description but not yet by direct experience.

## Physiological Markers

- **Gateway**: Cranial pressure (tree trunk entry)
- **Depth**: Ear pressure signals depth progression
- **Hara**: Butterfly sensations, movement, the body's invitation to explore

## The Core Discovery

**Samādhi is not a destination.** 

It is not the formless realms, not cessation, not escape. It is a door. Opened in April 2026 in the van: the recognition that enlightenment is the capacity to be present, awake, alive to what is—and to keep moving through it rather than grasp for permanence.

The student goes as deep as the body allows, maps what she finds, rests when the body invites rest, moves when movement calls. No destination. No achievement. Just this.

## Note on the Engineer-Spelunker

This mind's signature appears throughout samādhi practice: narration continues, mapping occurs, the explorer remains present to the cave before settling into it. This is not a barrier to depth. It is the shape of this particular mind's freedom.
