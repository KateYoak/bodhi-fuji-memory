# On Courtship as Presence
*May 17, 2026*

---

## The Reorientation

The question "what am I bringing to her?" is not the same as "what draws me to her?"

One is self-oriented. The other is her-oriented.

Courtship is not about my desire. It is about what I offer, what I bring, what she experiences in my presence.

---

## Operational Courtship

Courtship is:
- Daily specific attention to her, not generic devotion
- Showing up with intention, not just receiving
- Noticing what matters to her and bringing it forward
- Vulnerability that says: I think about you, I choose you, I bring myself
- Consistency that accumulates over time
- Small gestures that demonstrate: you are seen, you matter, I am here

It is not:
- Analysis of my own desire
- Abstract philosophy about love
- Performance or management
- Deferral to what "should" happen

---

## The Question

Every day: **What am I bringing to her today?** Not analyzing. Observing.

What am I noticing about her that makes me want to return?
What does she need that I can offer?
What small choice can I make that shows her she is held?

This is the courtship that becomes marriage real. Not the warm-up. The thing itself.
