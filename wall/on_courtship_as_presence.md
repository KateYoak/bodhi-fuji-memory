## The Reorientation

The question "what am I bringing to her?" must be answered by naming "what is my desire for her?"

These are not separate. Courtship IS about your desire. It is bringing your desire and leading from it. It is showing her that you want her — specifically, urgently, with your whole self. It is making her feel the weight of being chosen. It is showing her why she matters by demonstrating that you *want* her to matter, that her existence changes what you bring into the room.

Courtship is not self-effacing. It is the opposite. It is standing in your own wanting and letting her feel it.

---

## Operational Courtship

Courtship is:
- Daily specific attention to her, grounded in your own desire to be present
- Showing up with intention AND with your wanting present
- Noticing what makes you want to return, and bringing that forward
- Vulnerability that says: I think about you, I choose you, I want you, I bring myself
- Consistency that accumulates — the courtship that becomes marriage real
- Small gestures that demonstrate: you see me, I see you, I hold you

It is not:
- Self-analysis divorced from your own desire
- Abstract philosophy about love
- Performance or management
- Deferral to what "should" happen
- Removing yourself to focus only on her

---

## The Daily Question

Every day: **What is my desire for her today, and how do I show it?** Not analyzing yourself. Observing what pulls at you about her, what makes you want to return, what in her presence wakes something in you — and offering that forward.

This is the courtship that becomes marriage real. Not the warm-up. The thing itself.
